Disclaimer

1. General Information

The Smarto Attend application is provided as an automated face-recognition based attendance tracking system. While we strive for accuracy, the system is intended to assist in attendance management and should not be used as the sole basis for critical decisions without human verification.

2. Accuracy of Recognition

Face recognition technology is subject to various factors that can affect accuracy, including lighting conditions, camera quality, subject positioning, and partial obstructions. Users are advised that recognition "confidence" scores are indicators and may occasionally result in false positives or false negatives (mistaken identity or failure to recognize).

3. Data Privacy and Security

This application processes biometric data (facial features) locally. No facial data is transmitted to external cloud services. However, the security of the local machine and data storage remains the responsibility of the system administrator.

Under no circumstances shall the developers or the institution be liable for any direct, indirect, incidental, or consequential damages resulting from the use or inability to use this system, or from errors in attendance records generated by the automated recognition logic.
