Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At Smarto Attend, we respect the privacy of our students and staff. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data processed by the automated attendance system.

2. Data Collection

We collect the following types of information:

  • Biometric Data: Images captured during registration and used for live recognition. These are processed to create numerical facial embeddings.
  • Student Information: Name, Roll Number, and attendance logs (entry/exit times).
  • System Logs: Records of user login activity and administrative changes.

3. How We Use Data

Data is used exclusively for:

  • Automated attendance tracking.
  • Generating attendance reports for educational purposes.
  • System maintenance and security auditing.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data, including facial images and attendance records, is stored **locally** on the institution's server. We do not use third-party cloud storage for biometric data. Access is restricted to authorized administrative and teaching staff.

5. Data Retention

Biometric data is retained as long as the student remains enrolled in the institution or until manually deleted by an administrator. Attendance records are kept for the duration required by institutional policy.

For privacy concerns, please contact: admin@school.edu
